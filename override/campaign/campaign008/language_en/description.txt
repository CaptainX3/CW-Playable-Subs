1968:\nOvernight, the Soviet Union invades Czechoslovakia with 250,000 troops and 2000 tanks to crush the reformist government there. Seeing the complete lack of response by an America bound down by fighting Soviet-backed forces in Vietnam, an emboldened Brezhnev regime decide the time is ripe to increase the sphere of communist influence in Europe. Their target? West Germany.\nSoviet naval forces still rely on relatively obsolete surface warships, but cruise missile submarines are a threat to NATO convoys.
