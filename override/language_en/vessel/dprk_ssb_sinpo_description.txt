History=Self Noise Rating: 135 db\nEscape Depth: 400 ft\n\nThe Sinpo class submarine, also called the Gorae ("whale") or Pongdae class, is a new class of submarine produced in North Korea. Only one submarine has been observed in service. It is the largest submarine designed and built for the Korean People's Navy. There has been speculation the design is influenced by modern Russian submarines of Kilo class, or Golf class submarine, but the submarine is significantly smaller than these designs.
